Beverages are liquids to drink.

Its primary function is to quench thirst.

The word 'beverage' comes from the Latin word 'bevere,'

which means 'to rest after labor.

ABOUT

Many beverages are also derived from angiosperms;

these include coffee (Coffea arabica; Rubiaceae),

tea (Camellia sinensis; Theaceae),

many soft drinks

(e.g., root beer from the roots of Sassafras albidum; Lauraceae),

and most alcoholic beverages

(e.g., beer and whiskey from cereal grains and wine from grapes).
